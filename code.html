About the Exhibition

AURA is a transcendent journey through interconnected spaces, where ethereal light sculptures and ambient soundscapes create a deeply personal and atmospheric experience. Step into a world where light, sound, and space converge. Lose yourself in the art.

Key Experiences

AURORA

A dynamic canvas of shifting colors that dance in harmony with sound.

ECHO

An interactive void where your presence sculpts the auditory landscape.

VOID

Lose your sense of scale in an infinite expanse of shimmering particles.
